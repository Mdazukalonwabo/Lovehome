About Us

LOVEHOME has been connecting South Africa with their favourite furnishings for generations. That's why the vast majority of our costomers are also our friends. Drop into Love Home and chat with our knowledgable staff, learn how we produce top quality furniture at affordable prices. Talk about value!

Choose from our wide range of furniture and appliances from the bedroom to the kitchen, dining room to the lounge tailored for your taste and your budget. There are luxurios leather, imported fabrics and contemporary or classic design to choose from. It's all as a result of Love Home's unequalled experience in choosing stylish, quality furniture you can afford.

our History

celebrating 100 years of great service and quality

With history that spans 100 years of bring affordable and quality furniture and appliances to the people of South Africa. Love Home has carved itself into the heritage of our country. From humble beginnings in Gugulethu, during the gold rush in 1901, the supply of household goods and comforts have always been a necessity and thanks to David Love Home we will continue the tradition.

It's with the loyalty of everyday South Africans that we at Love Home are able to celebrate our 117th Birthday and etch our mark in history. Looking back serves as a reminder, not only of how far we have come but how far we still have to go.
